Physical

I don’t think I need to tell you the effects of not taking care of your physical health. In today’s times, we need to take care of our physical health even more because the average lifespan of a man has reduced, the food that we consume is not as pure and healthy as it was, and the life we lead has become sedentary. If you ignore your physical aspect of life, physical ailments will decrease the quality of your life.

Mental

If you are unaware of your mental well-being and completely ignore the mental aspect of your life, there are high chances of you developing a mental health condition. If not that you’re sure to lead a life full of stress and overpowering negative emotions.

Social

We are social beings and having good social relationships help us improve our quality of life. If you ignore the social aspect of your life, you’re soon going to feel isolated and lonely. This can heavily impact your mental health and also decrease your level of happiness in life.

Emotional

Our feelings, thoughts and emotions play a very important role in helping us lead a happy and fulfilled life. If you don’t manage, acknowledge and address your emotions time to time, they are going to bottle up an cause severe problems like attachment issues, neglect, emotional outburst, suicidal thoughts, etc.

Financial

Money is one of the most important aspects of life and has the power to affect all the other aspects of life because the world runs on money, for food, shelter, clothes, treatments, etc we need money. When your finances are not stable you are most likely to be under a lot of stress of managing different aspects of your life.
